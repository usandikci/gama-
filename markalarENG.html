Avikus

Avikus, as a subsidiary of HD Hyundai Group, is a world-leading technology company developing autonomous and AI-based solutions for the maritime industry. Founded in 2020, Avikus offers groundbreaking innovations in autonomous maritime technologies.

The company's technology combines artificial intelligence, sensor fusion, and advanced control algorithms to enable ships and boats to navigate more safely, efficiently, and intelligently. Avikus is one of the pioneering companies shaping the future of autonomous maritime.

Artificial Intelligence Autonomous Navigation Sensor Fusion Deep Learning Collision Avoidance

Technology Platforms

HiNAS (Intelligent Navigation Assistant)

AI-based navigation assistance system. Detects the vessel's surroundings 360 degrees, calculates collision risks in advance, and determines the optimal route.

HiNAS Control

Fully autonomous navigation control. Ability to navigate, dock, and maneuver on a determined route without any human intervention.

HiMAS (Intelligent Maintenance System)

AI-based maintenance assistant that monitors the condition of ship equipment and predicts potential failures in advance.

ZF Marine

ZF Friedrichshafen AG, founded in 1915, is one of the world's leading automotive and industrial technology suppliers. The ZF Marine division has been providing power transmission, steering, and control systems to the maritime industry for over 80 years.

ZF's marine solutions are known for reliability, durability, and high performance. Used in a wide range from commercial vessels to luxury yachts, workboats to military ships, ZF systems are indispensable components of maritime.

Transmission Pod Drive Joystick Control Power Transmission Fuel Efficiency

Key Technologies

ZF Pod Drives

Full-rotating pod systems, 360-degree maneuverability, up to 15% improvement in fuel efficiency, and low vibration levels.

Marine Transmissions

Mechanical and electronically controlled transmissions in a wide power range from 50 HP to 10,000 HP. High durability and quiet operation.

ZF Joystick Control

Precise one-hand maneuvering. Full integration with engines, pod, and thruster systems. Superior control in docking and tight maneuvers.
